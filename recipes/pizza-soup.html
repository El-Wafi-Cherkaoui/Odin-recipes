Pizza Soup

Description:

Pizza Soup is a tomato-based soup packed with flavors like pepperoni, Italian sausage, green peppers, and onion, and topped with cheese!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: This meaty pizza soup features sliced pepperoni (crisped and served on top) and bulk Italian sausage (in the soup itself).
  • Fresh produce: From the produce section, youâ€™ll need an onion and a green bell pepper.
  • Canned tomatoes: Canned diced tomatoes and canned tomato sauce give the soup a rich, flavorful base.
  • Broth: Use store-bought beef broth or make your own at home.
  • Seasoning: Use store-bought Italian seasoning or make your own at home.
  • Cheeses: Top each bowl of pizza soup with mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.

Steps:

  1. Cut the pepperoni slices in half, then crisp them on the stove. Drain on paper towels.
  2. Cook sausage in the same pot until cooked through.
  3. Add the fresh vegetables and cook until crisp-tender.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes, broth, and seasoning. Bring to a boil.
  5. Simmer, covered, for 10 minutes.
  6. Ladle the pizza soup into bowls and top with crisp pepperoni and cheeses.
